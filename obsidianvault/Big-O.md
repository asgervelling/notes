### Definition
$$
\begin{aligned}
f(n) &\in O(g(n)) \\
&\Leftrightarrow \\
\exists c \in \mathbb{R^+} \ 
\exists n_0 \in \mathbb{N} \ 
&:\ \forall n \geq n_0\ f(n) \leq cg(n)
\end{aligned}
$$
In other words, $f$ is upper bounded by $g$.
